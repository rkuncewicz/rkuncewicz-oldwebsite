ASECO Integrated Systems

General Info

During my first work term I was given the privelege to work at ASECO Integrated System, located at 635 Fourth Line, Unit 16 Oakville, Ontario. Founded in 1988 ASECO is currently a global leader in manufacturing consulting and systems integration. Their services include Manufacturing Consulting, Manufacturing Execusion Systems, and Controls Engineering. The primary industries they focus on are fiber materials, automative, consumer goods, food and beverage, steels, tobacco and much more.

ASECO works with partners such as Wonderware, GE, Rockwell, and Siemens using technologies which include ArchestrA, inTouch, RSLogix 5000 as well as other HMI and Control systems. Work at ASECO splits into ACS and MES, with the former dealing with the physical components on the lines and the latter dealing with reporting and data processing. ASECO is working in the United States, Canada, Great Britain, and expanding worldwide to meet the needs of customers around the world.

Department

I was put into the Controls Engineering department at ASECO learning about the integration services for PLC, DCS and SCADA applications including requirements, design, development, testinng, commissioning and training. Working with Wonderware, Rockwell and Modicon, Controls offers a deep knowledge of a wide variety of control and SCADA systems which are used in batch, discrete and continuous control processing.

Awards

ASECO Integrated Systems is a recipient of the Wonderwares Most certifications Globally and winner of the Control Engineering System Integrator of the Year.

Work At ASECO

Starting work at ASECO one quickly realizes that you are expected to catch up very quickly to the fast paced environment at the company. After basic training on concepts used in the company, you are put on a project quite quickly and are working with the members of your team right away. This at first can be a bit hectic, however you quickly realize that you must step up and work at everyones pace.
